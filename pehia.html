About Pehia

We’re creating a world where Gender is no longer a barrier to become successful in technology.


Pehia is a non-profit community envisioning to bridge the gender gap in technology and create an inclusive environment for students and professionals alike, to aid them in their journey. Pehia Foundation, being one of the most prestigious of it’s kind in kerala,India, have helped ace up the career of many enthusiastic bright minds through a chain of enlightening workshops, peer learning groups, online coding competitions, and provides mentorship for contenders in all levels to take part in hackathons together to drive solutions to products. Cherry on the top, we cater to you with opportunities like internships, jobs and scholarships from the best and provide you with a platform to showcase your tech-skills.


Mission: Pehia works to build relations among students and professionals, thereby helping to bridge the technical skill gap to form a space with the capacity to include everyone and anyone in the field of science and technology without the bias and discrimination in factors viz gender, status, financial ability,etc.


Vision: To serve and to be a prime ally of inclusivity and bias-free society to create a world where anyone can be free to learn and live up to their freedom.

Our Work

CodeIN

CodeIN is the monthly competitive coding competition that is hosted on the HackerRank platform to increase the coding aptitude, logical skills, basics of Data Structures and algorithms. CodeIN was launched on March 7, 2021, as a coding platform. Elite participants will be offered a chance to present a lightning talk at Pehia summit 2022.

K4Kode

K4Kode is an initiative to introduce young minds to STEM concepts and invoke in them the curiosity to learn new technology. Engaging students in STEM activities specially designed for them fosters creativity, out of box thinking, drive to find fresh and innovative solutions to existing challenges faced by various fields, in young people at a very tender age.

Labyrinth

Labyrinth - for the buildThirsty was brought by Pehia Foundation to help to hone the skills and give wings to the ideas that students have. Labyrinth welcomes new experiments and breakthroughs to help undergraduate students to complete their projects.

P2P

Peer learning is an educational practice in which students interact with other students to learn something new. Students learn a great deal by explaining their ideas to others and participating in activities in which they can learn from their peers.

Zee Camp

Pehia Foundation, for the past year, have been working towards bridging the gender gap in the tech field, with the focus on women in Kochi. The voyage over this arena has been successful with the ebb and flow of the diverse activities conducted so far. Albeit the success over the women-tech communities and the activities, Pehia had been thinking about the chances of extending our activities over to the transgender society, to remove the gender bias and boundaries in this field.
