About Beyond Born

Beyond Born Early Education Center is a specialized early childhood learning institution located in Palarivattom, Kochi (Kerala). It distinguishes itself by moving beyond traditional "daycare" models, focusing instead on a research-aligned, holistic approach to child development.


The center is designed as a space where foundational learning meets purposeful play, aiming to nurture curiosity, independence, and critical thinking in children during their most formative years.


Key Educational Philosophy


"How to Think" Model: Rather than rote memorization, the center emphasizes cognitive skills and problem-solving.


Child-Led Exploration: The environment encourages children to make age-appropriate choices and manage tasks independently.


Inclusive Environment: They provide focused support for children with special needs and those experiencing developmental milestone delays.


Multidisciplinary Team: The staff includes early childhood educators, child psychologists, and enrichment coaches to ensure emotional and social well-being alongside academic readiness.
