About KAP'O'

KAP’O’ Collective is a community-driven initiative dedicated to empowering educators and standardizing quality within the private Early Childhood Care and Education (ECCE) sector in Kerala. Prepared by Sreepriya Radhakrishnan and the Pethia Foundation, the collective aims to build a unified system where competition is replaced by shared wisdom and collaboration.


Our Mission

Our primary mission is to unify private ECCE providers across Kerala under a single standard of excellence. We strive to ensure every child has access to high-quality, play-based education that aligns with the National Education Policy (NEP) and the Kerala Curriculum Framework.
