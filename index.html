Stories and Thoughts

Hello, I'm Sreepriya.

A social entrepreneur and community builder based in Kochi, and I'm all about creating spaces where people—big or small—can grow.


Most of my days are spent running Beyond Born, where I focus on early childhood education and helping parents navigate the wild world of raising tiny humans. When I’m not at the preschool, you’ll likely find me working with the Pehia Foundation. As a Director there, I’m deeply committed to making the tech world more inclusive and helping women find their footing in the industry.


I’m a bit of a nerd for the intersection of technology, psychology, and design thinking. I love finding ways to automate the boring stuff so we can focus more on what actually matters: genuine human connection. Whether I’m building a community or exploring a new idea, I’m driven by the belief that learning should never stop.
